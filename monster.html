Bokoblins

Bokoblins and thier ilk are some of the most commonly found enemies across Hyrule. They come in a wide variety of colors, skills, and levels of intelligence. The one universal feature they all share is a complete and utter hatred for Hylians.

Moblins

Moblins once competed with Hylians for the dominance of Hyrule. Endless battles were fought between the two, but eventually the Hylians prevailed. Some say Demise punished the Moblin race for this failure by stealing thier wisdom, turning them into a race of near-mindless brutes.

Updated: 05/19/2020

Lizalfos

These chameleon-like creatures dominate most of Hyrule's waterways. They live in small tribes and live by hunting fish and crafting wares from driftwood and fish bone. They are fast and cunning creatures, and despise Zora because they claim territory they feel belongs to them.

Updated: 05/19/2020

Stalfos

The undead skeletons of Demise's spawn rise at night to attack any travellers they find, to little other end than mindless killing. Many believe that Demise is so obsessed with world-domination that he will not let his soliders sleep even in death.

Updated: 05/19/2020

Poe

While an undead being like Stalfos, a Poe is vastly different. Poe are intelligent, mischievous, and greedy, and have the unique ability to be able to shift between realms. Poe can't be killed, but if thier soul becomes trapped they cannot return to thier full self until freed.
